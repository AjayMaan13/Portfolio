About Me

Hi! I'm Ajaypartap Singh Maan — a passionate developer, problem solver, and lifelong learner. I'm currently studying Computer Programming & Analysis at Seneca Polytechnic, maintaining a 4.0 GPA and earning 3-time President's Honour List recognition.

I've been coding since I was 12, and I love turning complex ideas into practical solutions. My projects range from AI Receipt Analyzers using GPT-4 Vision, to real-time ETL pipelines for air quality monitoring, to secure C++ systems with memory-safe design.

With over 2,100 GitHub commits, I enjoy building across multiple domains — AI/ML, full-stack apps, data engineering, and cloud-based solutions. I'm curious about emerging technologies, always learning, and eager to collaborate on innovative projects that make an impact.

Outside of tech, you'll probably find me spiking a volleyball or diving into classic literature (currently Crime and Punishment by Dostoevsky).
